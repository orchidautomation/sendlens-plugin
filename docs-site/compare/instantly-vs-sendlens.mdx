# Instantly vs SendLens

Instantly is the system of record and system of action for Instantly campaigns. SendLens is the local intelligence layer that reads Instantly data and helps your AI host reason over it.

## Use Instantly for {#use-instantly-for}

- Creating and editing campaigns.
- Managing leads, sequences, sender accounts, and sending settings.
- Taking operational actions in the provider.

## Use SendLens for {#use-sendlens-for}

- Cross-campaign triage and prioritization.
- Evidence-backed copy, reply, lead, and sender analysis.
- Local DuckDB analysis inside an AI host.
- Normalized comparison with Smartlead workspaces when all-provider mode is configured.

## Commercial framing {#commercial-framing}

SendLens does not replace Instantly. It makes Instantly data easier to analyze, explain, and operationalize without exporting spreadsheets.
