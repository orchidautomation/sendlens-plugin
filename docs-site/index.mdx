# SendLens documentation

SendLens is the read-only outbound intelligence layer for AI hosts. It connects to outbound providers such as Instantly and Smartlead, normalizes campaign data into a local DuckDB cache, and gives your AI host evidence-backed answers about campaign health, replies, copy, lead quality, and launch readiness.

<Note>
SendLens is not a system of action. It does not create campaigns, send emails, mutate leads, update accounts, or manage webhooks. Native provider MCPs and provider apps are where actions belong; SendLens is where analysis, evidence, and cross-provider interpretation belong.
</Note>

## What SendLens helps you do

- Decide which campaigns to scale, pause, kill, or rewrite.
- Separate real buying signals from auto-replies and noisy inbox activity.
- Inspect copy risks such as empty bodies, unfilled tokens, and weak Step 0 angles.
- Analyze Instantly and Smartlead V1 workspaces through normalized read-only views, including all-provider comparison when both are configured.
- Explain every recommendation with evidence classes such as exact, sampled, reconstructed, inferred, or unsupported.
- Keep provider data local by default while using your existing AI host workflow.
